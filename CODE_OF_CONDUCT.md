# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume positive intent
- Focus discussion on code and behavior, not people
- Welcome newcomers and feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit comments/issues/PRs that violate these
standards and may restrict participation for repeated violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to maintainers.
